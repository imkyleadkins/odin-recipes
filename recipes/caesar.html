Caesar Salad Recipe

The Perfect Caesar Salad

Classic Caesar Salad with crisp homemade croutons and a light caesar dressing â€“ for when you want to impress your dinner guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 large or 2 small heads of romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved
  • Crisp croutons
  • Caesar salad dressing

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to large mixing bowl
  2. Add salad dressing to ingredients
  3. Toss gently to coat lettuce in dressing
  4. Add to serving bowls and enjoy
